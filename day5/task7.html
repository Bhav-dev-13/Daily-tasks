Butter Naan

Butter naan is a type of naan that is topped with nigella seeds and greased with butter. Naan is a flatbread that originated in Central and South Asia and is commonly eaten in India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Afghanistan. It is made from flour, leavening agent, salt, and butter or ghee, and is often baked in a tandoor see more.
