Now, where were we?

Rachel’s Coffee House is a new independent cafe founded by a pair of longtime Toronto coffee veterans. Maria Chan and her husband, Jack, owned a Second Cup franchise in the Eaton Centre for thirteen years. After years of confiding to the franchise guidelines, they decided it was time to take the plunge and go independent.

Serving up fresh coffee beverages made from locally roasted, organic, fair trade coffee beans. Delicious pastries, baked goods, and savoury sandwiches made from natural ingredients with few, if any, preservatives. A deceptively spacious location with plenty of seating and cozy front patio. These are the things that keep customers coming back.

We always use fresh organic ingredients We bake fresh croissants each morning in house, and have an assortment of gluten-free eats. We guarantee you won’t leave with an empty stomach!

Blended Drinks: We offer specialty blended drinks such as virgin pina coladas, strawberry mango smoothies, and vanilla creme drinks.

Espresso Drinks: We have a large variety of espresso drinks. Give one of our almond or matcha lattes a try!
